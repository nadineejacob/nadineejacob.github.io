an open letter to love

～ ～ ～

Dear, Love,

When I think of you, what comes to mind?

A hand to hold, a shoulder to cry on, a long hug after a bad day, laughing 'til you cry, your favorite song on repeat, adventures, friendships, promises, warmth, patience, family, trust, sacrifice, honesty, faith.

Though your presence is felt all year round, on this day, we celebrate you.
