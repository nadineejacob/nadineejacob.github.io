films to watch on valentine's day

Got no plans this Valentine's Day? Not to worry. Here are five romantic comedies you can watch alone, with your friends, or with your significant other to get in the true spirit of love.

1. Love, Rosie

Alex and Rosie have been best friends since they were 5 years old. After growing up together and experiencing the ups and downs of life side by side, a series of missed opportunities and wrong decisions send their lives in completely different directions. They always seem to find their way back to one another, but will they ever get the timing right?

2. What If

Med school drop-out Wallace meets animator Chantry at a party and the two instantly click. Despite the undeniable chemistry, they insist on just being friends. However, as their friendship grows more and more intimate over time, they both begin to wonder: what if your best friend is actually the love of your life?

3. Angus, Thongs, and Perfect Snogging

14-year old Georgia Nicholson is in a rush to grow up, go to parties, have her first kiss, and find the perfect boyfriend. The story follows Georgia and her friends as they learn and experience the joys and pains of being a teenager.

4. Stuck in Love

Bill Borgens, a well-renowned author, has not moved on from his wife who cheated on him and left him two years ago. His daughter, Sam, is a college undergraduate—a cynic, determined to avoid love at all costs; while her teenage brother, Rusty, also an aspiring writer, is an introvert and a hopeless romantic. The story follows each member of the family as they face the complexities and intricacies of love and learn that endings can often lead to new beginnings.

5. Flipped

Juli Baker has had the biggest crush on her neighbor, Bryce Loski, since the second grade. Bryce, unfortunately, doesn’t feel the same way and persistently avoids Juli as much as he possibly can. However, this all changes in the eighth grade, when Juli begins to realize that there are more important things in life than getting your crush to like you back, while Bryce begins to notice that Juli Baker isn’t like most girls.
